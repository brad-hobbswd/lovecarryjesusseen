What Love Was Carrying

Because Jesus Saw Everything

By Bradley Hobbs

A journey through love, faith, endurance, difficult seasons, and the unwavering presence of Jesus, who never looked away.

Begin Reading

Some burdens arrive suddenly, demanding immediate attention. Others settle quietly upon the heart, becoming so familiar that their weight no longer feels unusual. What Love Was Carrying explores the hidden burdens that often accompany love, faith, hope, disappointment, perseverance, and healing.

Through personal reflection and honest storytelling, this book follows a journey marked by questions, difficult realities, seasons of endurance, and the steady faithfulness of Jesus. Every chapter reveals what became visible when long carried burdens could no longer remain hidden and how the presence of Jesus remained constant through every moment.

This story is not about condemnation. This story is about truth, grace, healing, and the Savior who saw everything.
